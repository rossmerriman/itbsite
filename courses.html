These courses are all relavent/ modern courses that will lead students into fulfilling careers and this is all mosted on the modern up to date website ( see last picture on the carousel). These courses have evolved and been introduced over the years to accomidate students with interests in these fields. The first few courses being

  4. Business Studies, Information Technology and French
  5. - Business Studies, Information Technology and German

This was back in 1999 so it is easy to see how far ITB as a whole has progressed and modernized its courses. This is a key factor in the success in a college. Within this modernization additions such as(Information Technology with a Language, Horticulture, Applied Social Studies (Social Care) ) all being added in 2003. This was another big step in the expansion of ITB. A greater Selection of courses meaning a greater number of students, lecturers and facilities needed. Further devlopment was made in 2009 with the introduction of Creative Digital Media and Sports management and Coaching.
